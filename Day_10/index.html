What People Usually Asked

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does ProHealth offer?

ProHealth offers a wide range of services including emergency care, pediatrics, obstetrics & gynecology, cardiology, neurology, and psychiatry, ensuring comprehensive healthcare for all patients.

How do I schedule an appointment with ProHealth?

You can schedule an appointment with ProHealth by visiting our website and using the online booking form, or you can call our office directly at (123) 456-7890 to set up a visit.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, ProHealth accepts most major insurance plans. Please contact our billing department or check our website for a list of accepted providers.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your ID, insurance card, any relevant medical records, and a list of current medications to your appointment.

How do I request a prescription refill?

You can request a prescription refill by contacting our office via phone at (123) 456-7890 or through our online patient portal.
